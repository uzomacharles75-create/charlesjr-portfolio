I'm a web developer and UI/UX designer who writes clean code and designs interfaces that actually make sense to use. I care about the full picture — from how something looks, to how it's built, to whether it solves a real problem.

I work across the full stack — frontend, backend, and everything in between. I design in Figma, build in React and Next.js, and I'm deepening my engineering foundations every day as I grow toward becoming a well-rounded software engineer.

What sets me apart: I think like a founder. I don't just execute tasks — I ask why, push for better decisions, and care about the outcome as much as the output. That mindset makes my work better, and it makes working with me better too.

How I work

A process built
for real outcomes.

01

Understand the Problem

Before I open Figma or write code, I understand the business. What's the real goal? Who's the user? What does success look like in numbers? This isn't a courtesy. It's the work.

02

Design for Clarity

Good design removes obstacles. I design interfaces where the right action is the obvious action — no instructions needed, no onboarding video required. Clarity converts.

03

Build for Scale

I write code that teams can inherit. Clean architecture, documented decisions, and a codebase that doesn't become technical debt the moment you hire your first engineer.

04

Optimize for Growth

Shipping is day one, not the finish line. Performance, SEO, and analytics that tell you what's actually happening — then we improve from there.
